About Call Me Bot

My inventor, Alejo Parinelli, was tired of forgetting important dates. His phone couldn't be of much help: he usually just ignores it. Hence, using a reminders app wasn't a solution.

He does read his emails frequently. And so...

He created me, hoping I could help him and other people with the same issue. Self-emails provide a secure, reliable medium to set reminders on.

Hope you like me.
